# 🎬 Netflix Data Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive analysis of Netflix Movies and TV Shows using Python for data processing and exploratory analysis, and Power BI for interactive data visualization.

The objective of this project is to uncover meaningful insights into Netflix's content library, identify content trends, analyze genre popularity, evaluate ratings distribution, and understand how Netflix's catalog has evolved over time.

The project demonstrates the complete data analytics workflow, including data cleaning, feature engineering, exploratory data analysis (EDA), statistical analysis, and dashboard development.

---

## 🚀 Project Objectives

- Analyze the distribution of Movies and TV Shows on Netflix.
- Identify the most popular genres and content ratings.
- Examine content growth trends over the years.
- Discover top content-producing countries.
- Analyze content duration and age distribution.
- Build an interactive dashboard for business insights.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Content Distribution
- Movies constitute the majority of Netflix's content library.
- TV Shows represent a smaller but rapidly growing segment.

### Ratings Analysis
- TV-MA is the most frequently assigned content rating.
- Mature-audience content dominates the platform.

### Geographic Analysis
- The United States contributes the highest volume of Netflix content.
- India and the United Kingdom are among the leading content-producing countries.

### Genre Analysis
- Drama is the most popular genre on Netflix.
- International Movies and Documentaries also have significant representation.

### Growth Trends
- Netflix experienced substantial content expansion after 2015.
- Content additions peaked during the streaming boom years.

### TV Show Analysis
- Most TV Shows consist of 1–2 seasons.
- Long-running series represent a smaller percentage of the catalog.
